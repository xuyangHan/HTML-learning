CSB Project [Web-Portal]

Crowd-Sourced Bathymetry in the Northern Canada areas


Crowd-Sourced Bathymetry (CSB) is envisioned by a certain number of International Hydrographic Organization (IHO) member states to supplement hydrographic databases with measurements from ships of opportunity. This platform provides online access, visualization, spatial analysis and data sharing services of CSB data to give diverse perspectives of water in the Northern Canada areas. The project is aimed at contributing to Bathymetry research. We trust this platform will be helpful to our targeted audience including, but not limited to, decision makers, local communities, governments, and researchers.
